Increasing the Range of Your USB WiFi Adapter?

Most of the WiFi Routers and USB WiFi Adapter allow you to change the inbuilt antenna. Usually, the inbuilt WiFi adapter antenna that are provided by the manufacturer will be having 5 dbi or 7 dbi or some times upto 9 dbi power gain, which is very low when compared to the currently available antenna. So if your USB WiFi adapter does support an external antenna, you can get a higher gain antenna like a 10 dbi or a 28 dbi. That will surely increase WiFi range.
